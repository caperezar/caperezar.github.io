Hello and welcome! I am an Assistant Professor (UD2 with tenure) in the Mathematics of Computational Science group (MACS) within the Department of Applied Mathematics at the University of Twente, The Netherlands.

My research lies in the intersection of wave phenomena, scientific computing, integral equations, high-order PDE solvers, and numerical analysis. More detailed information about my research is available here.
